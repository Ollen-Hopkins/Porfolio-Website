Front End Developer

My passion for Software began in 2019, with game development using Unity Engine, Microsoft Visual Studio IDE, and Youtube tutorials. Like many self-taught Game Developers, I had no sense of direction during the learning process. It was all about taking in and retaining as much information as needed based on job requirements. Ultimately imposter syndrome won over me for a while. Fast Forward July 2022, so 9 months since I've typed one line of code. I discover nucamp an affordable coding bootcamp with a proven track record. Finally, I found my sense of direction for what companies are looking for. Based on When you're reading this I will be attending or will have finished "Front-end Web and Mobile App Development Bootcamp."
